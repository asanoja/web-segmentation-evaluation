Cars, Trucks, SUVs for Sale

All of the cars for sale at Enterprise Car Sales have no-haggle pricing, which makes it easier to compare and find car sales online with the best prices. When you visit our used car lots, you will appreciate our professional car dealerships and our low-pressure car sales approach that provides all the vehicle specs and details on rental cars for sale.

When you buy a car from Enterprise, you get our 7-day repurchase agreement1, so if you change your mind afterward, we'll buy a car back with no questions asked. Our car dealers will give you up to 7 days or 1,000 miles to change your mind on cars for sale. Plus, our certified used cars come with a 109-point vehicle certification, 12/12 limited powertrain warranty2, and 12-month unlimited roadside assistance3.

Our inventory of cars, trucks, hybrids and SUVs for sale are selected mostly from the Enterprise Rent-A-Car fleet to offer unsurpassed quality and value, with a wide selection that includes: Chevy, Honda, Ford, Toyota, Dodge, Nissan, Hyundai, Kia, Chrysler, Mazda, Mitsubishi, Jeep, Volkswagen, GMC, Buick, BMW, Mercedes, Cadillac, Lincoln, Volvo, Infiniti, Lexus, Acura, Audi, Subaru, Fiat and many other automobiles. Check our auto sales offers online to compare our cheap car prices, including cars for sale under $15,000.
